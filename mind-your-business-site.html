ü§î Common Boundary Violations

These seemingly innocent questions can make people uncomfortable:


"When are you having kids?" - Maybe they can't, don't want to, or it's none of your business

"Why are you still single?" - Implies something's wrong with being single

"How much do you make?" - Financial details are personal

"Why don't you drink?" - Could be health, addiction, or personal reasons

"Are you pregnant?" - Never, ever ask this

üéØ The Solution

Ask yourself first: "Do I need to know this information? Will knowing this actually help me support this person better?"


If someone wants to share, they will. Your job is to be a safe person to share with, not an interrogator.


Show interest without prying: "How are you doing?" is much better than "Why haven't you found a new job yet?"
